# Pika 2.0 Prompting Guide

Category: Video

## Official Guidance Highlights
- No official Pika prompt guide was accessible via automated fetch.
- Use short, focused prompts with a single action and clear motion.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Subject + action
2. Scene and lighting
3. Camera motion
4. Style or tone

## Positive Examples
- "A paper boat floats in a rain puddle, the view drifts alongside, soft overcast light, gentle ripples."
- "Close-up of hands kneading dough, slow push-in, warm kitchen light, subtle steam."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "A character does three different actions" (too much motion)
- "Make it cinematic" (no concrete details)
