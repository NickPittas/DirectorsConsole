# Qwen-Image Prompting Guide

Category: Image

## Official Guidance Highlights
- Qwen-Image emphasizes strong text rendering and precise image editing.
- Use clear, detailed prompts with explicit text in quotes when needed.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Subject + action
2. Scene and setting
3. Lighting and mood
4. Style or medium
5. Text in quotes with placement (if required)

## Positive Examples
- "Product hero shot of a matte black water bottle on a stone surface, soft daylight, subtle shadow."
- "A cafe sign that reads "MOONLIGHT" in bold white letters, centered, warm amber lighting, night street scene."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "Text somewhere" (unclear placement and style)
- "Beautiful, high quality" (vague adjectives)

## Notes
- Keep text short for higher accuracy.
