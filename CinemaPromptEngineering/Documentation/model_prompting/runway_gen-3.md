# Runway Gen-3 Prompting Guide

Category: Video

## Official Guidance Highlights
- Official Runway guides were not accessible via automated fetch (403). Refer to Runway Help Center for the latest prompt guidance.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Camera framing and motion
2. Subject + action
3. Environment and lighting
4. Style or tone

## Positive Examples
- "Handheld close-up of a drummer, shallow depth of field, the frame sways subtly with the beat, warm stage lights."
- "Wide shot of a rain-soaked street, slow dolly forward, neon reflections, noir tone."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "Make it cinematic" (no camera or action details)
- "Scene 1 beach, scene 2 city" (multi-scene in short clip)
