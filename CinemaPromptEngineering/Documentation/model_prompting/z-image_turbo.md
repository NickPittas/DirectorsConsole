# Z-Image Turbo Prompting Guide

Category: Image

## Official Guidance Highlights
- Use clear subject and layout details; keep prompts focused.
- For text, quote the exact wording and describe placement.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Subject + action
2. Scene and setting
3. Lighting and mood
4. Style or medium
5. Text in quotes with placement (optional)

## Positive Examples
- "Minimal logo mockup: the word "NORTH" in clean uppercase sans, centered on a pale blue field."
- "A minimalist product shot of a white ceramic mug, soft diffused light, neutral background."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "Make it pretty" (no visual anchors)
- "Photo, watercolor, oil" (conflicting styles)
