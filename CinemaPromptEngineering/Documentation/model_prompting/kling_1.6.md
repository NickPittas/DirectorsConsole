# Kling 1.6 Prompting Guide

Category: Video

## Official Guidance Highlights
- The official Kling prompt guide could not be fetched automatically. Use the Kling prompt guide in the app when available.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Subject + action
2. Scene and lighting
3. Camera framing and motion
4. Style or tone

## Positive Examples
- "Medium shot of a chef plating pasta, slow push-in, warm kitchen light, soft steam."
- "Wide shot of a foggy forest trail, gentle pan right, cool palette, quiet ambience."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "Make it cool" (vague)
- "Multiple locations" (too many scene changes)
