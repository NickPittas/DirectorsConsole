# Runway Gen-4 Prompting Guide

Category: Video

## Official Guidance Highlights
- Official Runway guides were not accessible via automated fetch (403). Refer to Runway Help Center for the latest prompt guidance.

## Recommended Prompt Structure
1. Camera framing and motion
2. Subject + action
3. Environment and lighting
4. Style or tone

## Positive Examples
- "Smooth crane up over a city plaza at sunset, warm light, soft haze, cinematic grade."
- "Medium shot of a chef plating food, slow push-in, warm kitchen light, gentle steam."

## Negative Examples (What to Avoid)
- "Epic video" (no shot details)
- "Many quick cuts" (unclear action and timing)
